Parking Lot Safety Protocol

Please watch the training video below and review the key safety points before marking as complete.

Key Safety Points

  • Stay Visible: Wear your high-vis vest at all times in active lots.
  • Speed Kills: Maintain a 5-10 MPH limit; watch for pedestrians between cars.
  • Secure the Area: Use cones to mark your workspace—don't trust drivers to see you.
